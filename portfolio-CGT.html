Project Information

  • Category: CAD
  • Class: CGT 163: Graphical Communication and Spatial Analysis
  • Project Dates: 08/22/2022 - 12/10/2022

Description

In this course, I learned about the process of 3D geometric modeling and construction techniques used in the creation of constraint-based solid and surface models. I also learned how to use the design process as part of the problem solving methodology to capture design intent and parameterize models. Overall, this course taught me the importance of Computer Aided Design in the product design process and how to use PDM systems to monitor the evolution of a product. I made numerous models during this class ranging from a basic coupler to advanced assemblies like a flat-bed shopping cart. It all culminated in a final project where we modeled a small engine from scratch. This required many parts to be created separately and multiple different assemblies to achieve the final product.
